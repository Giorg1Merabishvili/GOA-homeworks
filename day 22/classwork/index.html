Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo was born on April 15, 1452 in Vinci, a small town in the Republic of Florence (modern-day Italy).
He was the illegitimate son of a notary, Ser Piero, and a peasant woman named Caterina. From an early age,
Leonardo showed a passion for art and curiosity about the natural world. At around age 14, he became an apprentice to the famous artist
Andrea del Verrocchio in Florence, where he learned painting,
sculpture, and mechanical arts.
