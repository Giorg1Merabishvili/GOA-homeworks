Men's Pound-for-Pound Top Rank

Islam Makhachev

Islam Ramazanovich Makhachev, born on October 27, 1991,
in Makhachkala, Dagestan, Russia, is a prominent Russian mixed martial artist and former combat sambo
competitor. He currently competes in the Lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC),
where he holds the title of UFC Lightweight Champion.

Jon Jones

Jonathan Dwight "Jon" Jones (born July 19, 1987) is an American professional mixed martial artist,
widely regarded as one of the greatest fighters in UFC history. He currently competes in the Heavyweight
division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), where he is the reigning UFC Heavyweight Champion.
Jones previously held the UFC Light Heavyweight Championship from 2011 to 2015 and again from 2018 to 2020,
as well as the interim title in 2016. As of November 19, 2024, he is ranked #2 in the UFC men's pound-for-pound rankings.

Ilia Topuria

Born on January 21, 1997, in Halle, Germany, to Georgian parents who were refugees from Abkhazia,
Topuria moved to Georgia at the age of seven. There, he began practicing Greco-Roman wrestling alongside his brother,
Aleksandre. At 15, the family relocated to Alicante, Spain, where Topuria immersed himself in martial arts training at the Climent Club.
In 2018, he and his brother became the first Georgians to earn black belts in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
